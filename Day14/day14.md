# Day 14 – AI Red Flag Detector for Job Seekers

## Objective
Used AI to analyze a Software Developer Intern job description and company information to identify potential hiring risks, workplace red flags, and positive signals before applying.

## What I Did
- Analyzed the job description using Claude AI.
- Identified unrealistic requirements and excessive qualifications.
- Evaluated workplace culture and burnout indicators.
- Checked remote work authenticity.
- Reviewed hiring and company-related risks.
- Generated smart interview questions to validate concerns.

## Key Findings
- Intern role asking for 1–2 years of experience is a potential red flag.
- "Fast-paced startup" and "wear multiple hats" may indicate increased workload.
- Remote role with occasional office meetings suggests a hybrid expectation.
- Salary information was not provided.
- Positive signals include mentorship, learning opportunities, PPO, and internship certification.

## Overall Verdict
⚠️ Apply with Caution

## Skills Practiced
- Prompt Engineering
- AI-powered Job Analysis
- Critical Thinking
- Hiring Risk Assessment
- Career Research

## Key Learning
AI can help evaluate job opportunities beyond the job title by identifying hidden red flags, assessing company culture, and generating interview questions that enable informed career decisions.
