# Day 16 – Build Your First Stock Research Skill

## Objective
Created a reusable Custom Skill in Claude for fundamental stock research and tested it on multiple Indian listed companies.

## Skill Name
**stock-fundamental-research**

## Description
A reusable Claude Custom Skill that analyzes Indian and global listed companies using fundamentals, financial statements, valuation metrics, business quality, ownership trends, competitive advantages, risks, and growth prospects.

---

## What I Built

Created a custom skill capable of:

- Quick Take analysis
- Deep Dive reports
- Stock Comparison
- Pros & Cons analysis
- Portfolio Fit analysis

The skill automatically structures financial analysis while avoiding investment recommendations.

---

## Tested Stocks

- Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)
- Infosys
- Reliance Industries
- HDFC Bank
- Tata Motors

---

## Outputs Generated

### Deep Dive
- Company Snapshot
- Valuation Analysis
- Growth Analysis
- Financial Health
- Returns
- Peer Comparison
- Ownership Pattern
- Overall View

### Compare Mode

Compared:

**TCS vs Infosys**

Metrics Included:

- Market Cap
- PE Ratio
- PB Ratio
- ROE
- ROCE
- Debt
- Dividend Yield
- Revenue Growth
- Profit Growth

---

### Pros & Cons Mode

Generated evidence-based strengths and risks for:

- Reliance Industries
- HDFC Bank
- Tata Motors

---

## Key Learnings

- Claude Custom Skills can eliminate repeatedly writing long prompts.
- One reusable skill can generate consistent research reports.
- Fundamental investing requires validating data from multiple sources.
- Corporate actions such as mergers or demergers can affect historical comparisons.
- Structured outputs improve readability and analysis quality.
