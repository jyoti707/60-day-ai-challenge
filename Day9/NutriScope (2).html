Please read

Educational Disclaimer

NutriScope is an educational and informational tool only. All calorie, macronutrient and micronutrient values are approximate averages drawn from public nutrition references and typical serving sizes — actual values vary by brand, preparation method, and ingredient quality.

Energy and nutrient targets are estimated using standard population formulas (Mifflin-St Jeor equation) and general RDA/AI reference values. They do not account for individual medical conditions, pregnancy, lactation, medications, or lab-confirmed deficiencies.

Nothing in this app constitutes medical, dietetic, or clinical advice, and the Risk Analysis and Recommendations sections are rule-based educational indicators, not a diagnosis or treatment plan. Always consult a registered dietitian, nutritionist, or physician before making significant changes to your diet, especially if you are managing a health condition.
Reference basis

Nutrition Data Sources

The food database and daily targets in NutriScope are approximated from the following types of public references.

USDA FoodData Central

Primary reference for macronutrient and micronutrient composition of common raw and cooked foods (calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber, vitamins and minerals per 100g).

Indian Food Composition Tables (IFCT 2017), ICMR–National Institute of Nutrition

Used as a reference point for South Asian staples such as dals, rotis, rice dishes and regional vegetables that are underrepresented in Western food databases.

ICMR-NIN Nutrient Requirements for Indians (RDA/AI, 2020)

Basis for age- and gender-adjusted target values for iron, calcium, vitamin C, zinc and other micronutrients shown in this app.

NIH Office of Dietary Supplements — Fact Sheets

General reference for typical adult Recommended Dietary Allowances (RDA) for vitamin A, folate, B12, vitamin D, magnesium and zinc.

WHO/FAO Guidelines on Sodium and Potassium Intake

Basis for the sodium upper-limit and potassium adequate-intake reference values used in the nutrient table and risk analysis.
