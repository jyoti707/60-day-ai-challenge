## Market Demand Insights
MERN and Node-based full-stack internships are actively posted and in steady demand in the Delhi-NCR/Bangalore corridor right now — you're searching in a healthy market for your stack.
Compensation reality check: the internship market for your experience level (student, pre-graduation) skews heavily toward ₹5,000–15,000/month stipends, not LPA-scale compensation. 4–8 LPA is realistic for a full-time entry-level SDE role post-graduation, not typically for a current internship — you may want to separate these two goals (internship now vs. placement target for 2027).
Startups (CashKaro, nxtrole/Neith, Praetor) are more willing to give real ownership and mentorship early, while IT services companies (OtusOne) tend to offer more structured but process-heavy exposure.
Several listings mention "internship-to-FTE conversion" as a real pathway — worth prioritizing those when applying, since they double as a foot in the door for full-time hiring later.
